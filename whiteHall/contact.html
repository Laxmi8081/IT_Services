Certified Lean Six Sigma Green Belt (CLSSGB) Program | October 2023
October 6 @ 1:00 pm – October 28 @ 5:00 pm

The Lean Six Sigma Green Belt is the key change agent in leading organizational and financial improvement where it is needed most. They lead process improvement projects within their department or function. Green Belts are part-time project managers with a functional knowledge of Lean Six Sigma tools and methodology.

What’s unique with Whitehall’s program?

  • Emphasis on project-based learning. More actual and on-the-job practice. Less academic and theoretical treatment of the subject.
  • Grounded on the universal principles of leading change, innovation, and sound project management practices.
  • Facilitated by ASQ Certified Master Black Belt with experience in deploying Lean Six Sigma in Fortune 500 companies.
  • Designed and tested by seasoned practitioners with more than 100 years of combined professional experience with a single focus – to enhance the chance of success for the aspiring Green Belt candidate.

This unique program focuses on the “why” do it and “how” to do your respective projects. Whitehall’s 90-day program for Green Belts will equip you with the skills and techniques to complete your project in record time.
