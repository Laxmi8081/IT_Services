About Us

Whitehall is a road in the City of Westminster, in central London, recognized as the center of Her Majesty's Government. Dubbed as “The street that shaped a nation”, Whitehall Consulting in a similar way, aims to shape the Philippines' consultancy landscape by pursuing excellence, one company at a time. Our logo embodies the same mission with the yellow triangular design element pointing upward to signify growth, maturity, and improvement.

With our consultants’ exquisite credentials and proven experience in accelerating the competency of professionals, Whitehall is one of the forefront training and consulting companies providing best practice solutions and sustained application of learning.

Vision

We aim to be ASEAN’s leading partner in developing globally-competitive experts.

Mission

We enable our partners to create sustainable business value by providing them best practice solutions.
